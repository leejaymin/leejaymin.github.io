This project is an international collaborative research effort between Jeonbuk National University and the University of Colorado. The objective is to develop quantization kernel code that enables efficient low-precision computation for deep learning workloads. Through this collaboration, the two institutions aim to design and implement optimized quantization kernels that can be integrated into modern AI inference pipelines.
